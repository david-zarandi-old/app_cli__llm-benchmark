 Tanya had just left her car parked on the street when she saw smoke rising from it. She quickly called the fire department, and they arrived shortly after with their truck full of firefighters. While everyone was watching in horror at the flames engulfing Tanya's beloved vehicle, Bruce, a police officer who handled traffic accidents, came to her rescue. Despite his experience seeing terrible things on accident scenes, he knew how important it was to remain calm for Tanya and immediately recognized her car when he arrived.
